Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our payment processor.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remaining time in your billing cycle. Downgrades will take effect at your next billing date.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for all our plans with no credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during your trial period.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

We're happy to support non-profit organizations with a 25% discount on all plans. Please contact our sales team with proof of your non-profit status to get set up with this discount.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all annual plans. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, we'll refund your payment in full. Monthly plans can be canceled at any time with no further charges.

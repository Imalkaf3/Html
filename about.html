Our Story

Discover the people, values, and passion behind Lumina's innovative solutions that are transforming businesses worldwide.

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate technologists, Lumina started as a small startup with a big vision: to make cutting-edge digital solutions accessible to businesses of all sizes.

What began as a three-person operation in a shared workspace has grown into an industry-leading company with offices in three countries and a team of over 150 dedicated professionals.

Our journey hasn't always been easy, but our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has remained constant. Every challenge we've faced has only strengthened our resolve to deliver exceptional solutions.
